Senior cosmologist at the Spanish National Research Council, working at the frontier of observational cosmology — from the Cosmic Microwave Background to the large-scale structure of the universe. Builder of PLANCK, DES, PAU, and Euclid. Member of DESI and LSST.

The first physical evidence for the existence of dark energy, independent of supernovae data.
