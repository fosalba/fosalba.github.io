Research Vision & ERC Scientific Program

Towards Precision Cosmology of the Dark Universe
My research program aims to establish a predictive theoretical and computational framework for the formation of large‑scale structure in the Universe, enabling robust cosmological inference from next‑generation surveys. The long‑term objective is to connect galaxy formation physics, dark matter dynamics, and cosmological initial conditions through ultra‑large simulations and novel statistical methodologies. This program directly addresses frontier questions in cosmology: the nature of dark energy, the origin of cosmic structure, and the limits of the standard cosmological model.
